Stock market crash today: BSE Sensex and Nifty50, the Indian equity benchmark indices, tanked in trade on Thursday. While BSE Sensex gave up the 83,000 level, Nifty50 went below 25,300. At 2:01 PM, BSE Sensex was trading at 82,584.35, down 1,682 points or 2%. Nifty50 was at 25,285.05, down 512 points or 1.98%. Sensex and Nifty50 mirrored losses in other Asian markets as investors reduced their risk appetite amidst the escalating conflict in the Middle East.
